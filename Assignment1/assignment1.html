Perfect Popcorn

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup popcorn kernels
  • olive oil
  • 1-2 pinches of salt

Directions:

  1. Add oil, salt, and popcorn to a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Cover with a lid
  3. Occasionally shimmy the pot to combine the oil, salt, and popcorn while keeping the lid on
  4. Continue to cook until the popping slows down
  5. Remove from heat and transfer to a bowl
  6. Enjoy!
