About Us

Come to the Purrfect Cat Cafe to have delicious coffee and snacks while you pet and cuddle our adorable feline friends. In our cozy environment, you and the kitties will feel just at home with a variety of seating options and cat toys. Whether you want to enjoy some quiet reading time, get some work done, play with cats, or enjoy delicious food, the Purrfect Cat Cafe is the place for you.

Since we have partnered with the Leyden Humane Society, every cat in our cafe is available for adoption. At the cafe, you can learn about each of the cat’s personalities and inquire about the adoption process. There is no obligation to adopt a cat when visiting the Purrfect Cat Cafe, however, the option is there if you fall in love with one of our feline friends.

We try our best to cater to all visitors with our vegan and gluten free options. Our menu has hot and cold drinks, sandwiches, and freshly baked pastries.
